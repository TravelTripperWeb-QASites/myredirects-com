Weddings & Wedding Blocks

Hotel Giraffe is a stunning backdrop for the most special day of your life, and we would be honored to be your hosts. Whether your plans for your wedding in New York City are to arrange guest accommodations, a rehearsal dinner, or a full ceremony and reception, Hotel Giraffe's experienced staff will ensure that all of your expectations and special requests are surpassed. Our Ceremony Fee includes: Chiavari Ballroom Chairs with cushions, set-up, breakdown, overnight guestroom accomodations for the happy couple (located convenientely on the 12th floor), and a Prosecco toast for the wedding party pre and post ceremony. For a full reception, Hotel Giraffe offers a variety of seated and buffet menu options as well.
